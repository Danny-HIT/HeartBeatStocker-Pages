Privacy Policy

HeartBetter is designed so activity data stays on Apple Watch and Apple Health.

Data Use

HeartBetter uses HealthKit on Apple Watch to read heart-rate and workout data for live activity cues and recent in-app history. When "Save to Health" is enabled, the app can save completed workouts to Apple Health.

HeartBetter does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store your health data on developer servers. Health data stays on your Apple Watch and in Apple Health.

No Accounts or Tracking

  • No account system.
  • No analytics.
  • No advertising.
  • No tracking.
  • No third-party SDKs.
  • No developer servers or app-side data networking.

Local Settings

The app stores settings on device, such as heart-rate ranges, selected workout type, vibration options, session timer preference, and Save to Health preference.

Local settings remain on the Apple Watch until you change or reset them, or remove the app. Clearing shown in-app history stores only local workout identifiers so those sessions remain hidden; it does not store heart-rate values.

User Control

You can revoke Health access at any time in Apple Health privacy settings. You can delete saved workouts from Apple Health. You can also clear shown in-app history from HeartBetter; clearing shown history does not delete Apple Health data. Removing the app deletes its local settings and hidden-history identifiers.

Fitness-Only Notice

HeartBetter provides fitness guidance only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, emergency monitoring, treatment, or a substitute for a clinician.
